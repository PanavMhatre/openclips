## Winning Hook Formulas (proven by data)

1. **Scale-vs-expectation ratio:** "[Famous thing] outnumbers / outperforms [expected thing] by [shocking ratio]" → Lobbyists 35:1 (1,403 YT views)
2. **ALL-CAPS + IS + emotional adjective:** "TIME DILATION IS REAL AND MIND-BENDING" → template that works on YouTube and IG for science content
3. **Country/asset + crash + consequence for you:** "[Country]'s [crash/problem] is a warning for the whole world" → 1,195 YT views
4. **Named person + financial disaster verb + dollar amount:** "[Name] WIPES OUT $[B]" → 337 TikTok views, 25,702ms IG watch time
5. **Counterintuitive path framing:** "Why [conventional wisdom] is [wrong / a trap]" → entrepreneurship + VC content
6. **NVIDIA risk/decision origin:** "Why NVIDIA [gave up / almost died] building [thing]" → IG #1 post (878 views)
7. **Insider reveal (named subject):** "[Speaker] reveals that [common thing] [hidden fact]" — must name the speaker

- **TikTok reality check:** With 3 followers, all TikTok views come from the For You algorithm. Content that matches TikTok's political/economic/controversy appetite gets 100-337 views when mature. Startup/VC/NVIDIA content gets 1-4 views regardless of age. Stop posting those there.
- **Re-cut cap:** Same topic across multiple cuts drops fast — "1% Secret" went 530→285→234→214→198→152→138→123 across 8 cuts. Max 2-3 cuts per topic per 30-day window.
- **ALL-CAPS titles:** Science/tech content posted with ALL-CAPS titles ("TIME DILATION IS REAL", "MODIFYING ATOMIC NUCLEI CAN RELEASE ENORMOUS ENERGY") consistently hits 1,000+ on YouTube. This may be a title-format signal worth A/B testing on other categories.
- **Named subjects outperform generic topics:** "Lobbyists Outnumber Elected Officials 35 To 1" beats "Why Government Is Broken." Always name the thing, person, or number.
- **Account size context:** At 15 IG followers and 3 TikTok followers, every view is algorithm-driven. The strategy is entirely about matching the algorithm's topic appetite on each platform, not building audience loyalty yet.
