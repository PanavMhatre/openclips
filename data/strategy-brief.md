## Winning Hook Formulas (use these exact structures)

1. "Why [respected person / company] [did controversial thing]" → highest saves on IG
2. "How [shocking number] [changes a fundamental assumption]" → e.g., 4 trillion parameters, 50 GW, $300M
3. "[Expert] explains the [flaw / secret / hidden truth] behind [widely-trusted thing]"
4. "Why [conventional wisdom] is [wrong / a trap / overrated]" → entrepreneurship + VC content
5. "The [number]% [secret / rule / thing] that [person type] never talks about" → Codie-style insider reveal

## Notes

- **TikTok data lag:** All TikTok clips posted within 48h show 0-4 views at sync time — this is platform latency, not failure. Evaluate TikTok performance at 5+ days post-publish only.
- **Multiple cuts strategy:** "Why Suffering Beats Business School" accumulated ~1,500 YT views across 3 different cuts. Running 2-3 cuts of top-performing topics from different angles or timestamps is a reliable volume multiplier — continue this practice for NVIDIA and Codie topics.
- **Save count as signal:** On Instagram, saves (not just likes) predict future algorithmic reach. Posts with 5+ saves ("Why NVIDIA Gave Up Profits for CUDA" had 7) should be boosted or recycled as cover clips.
- **YouTube Shorts vs long-form note:** All current YT content is Shorts-length. The channel has 26 subscribers and 39,988 total views on 102 videos — averaging ~392 views/video. Top clips (700+) are 80% above average, confirming the NVIDIA and entrepreneurship clusters are genuine outliers worth pressing.
- **Avoid duplicate-angle fatigue:** "The 1% Secret Every Seller Hides" appeared in 5 separate cuts — engagement drops off sharply after the first 2 (530 → 285 → 234 → 214 → 198 → 123). Cap topic re-cuts at 2-3 per 30-day window.
